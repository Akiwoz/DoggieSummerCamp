At our summer camp we offer more than just a day beside the pool!

One on one


We understand some dogs may have issues with other dogs or just have trouble being with many people and dogs in one place. We offer one on one fun with a doggie camper so they don't miss all the fun and get all the love as if they're were in a group with the others!

Spa


With our spa days your best friend is given the most best of the best with a pool day, a nice shower, hair cut if you would like, massage, and the best of the best treats before, during, and right before bed. (twice a week Mon and Friday)

Training


We're not just a summer camp, but we also include training for your best friends during the whole stay they are there with certified professionals. (twice a week for 2 hours with one on one training or group training) .
